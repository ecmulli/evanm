# Job Search Criteria

## About Me
Lead Analytics Engineer at Livepeer Inc (Series A/B). 8.5 years in data -- data science
background, now analytics engineering and data leadership. Specialty in real-time data
streaming (Kafka, ClickHouse), building data functions from scratch, and cross-functional
partnership with directors/executives to translate business needs into data strategy.
Experienced in team building, hiring, and standing up analytics orgs at early-stage
companies. Background in teaching (math, Graduate TA of the Year) shapes how I communicate
complex ideas and develop people. Recently went through a reduction at Livepeer.

## Target Roles

### Director / Head Track (smaller companies, early-stage)
- Head of Data, Head of Analytics
- Director of Data, Director of Analytics
- Director of Data & Analytics
- VP of Data, VP of Analytics

### IC Track (larger or more established companies)
- Analytics Engineer (any level: Senior, Staff, Principal, Lead)
- Founding Analytics Engineer
- Lead Analytics Engineer
- Senior Analytics Engineer

## Must-Haves (Dealbreakers -- score 1 if missing)
- Remote or remote-first (occasional travel OK)
- Base salary >= $180k (if listed; don't penalize if salary not posted)
- One of the target role titles above (or clearly equivalent)
- NOT requiring specific regulated-industry experience I don't have (healthcare compliance, fintech regulations, government clearance)

## Strong Positive Signals (weight heavily in scoring)
- "First data hire" / "building the data function" / "from scratch" / "founding"
- Modern data stack: dbt, BigQuery, Snowflake, ClickHouse, or similar cloud DW
- Real-time or streaming analytics: Kafka, event-driven architecture, ClickHouse
- AI/ML integration, modern tooling (Claude, LLMs, AI-assisted analytics, MCP)
- Cross-functional leadership: partnering with product, engineering, execs
- Startup language: ownership, autonomy, move fast, wear many hats
- Team building or hiring responsibility (for director-track roles)
- Series A through D, ~20-500 employees

## Nice-to-Haves
- A/B testing, experimentation platforms
- Data modeling, analytics engineering explicitly mentioned
- Video, media, streaming, or infrastructure/platform domain
- Developer tools or dev-focused company
- Self-service analytics, data democratization

## Red Flags (deprioritize)
- Tableau, Excel, or legacy BI as primary tools -- signals slow-moving analytics culture
- Enterprise / Fortune 500 (too much process, not enough ownership)
- Heavy MLOps or ML platform focus (not my primary strength)
- On-site or hybrid-only with no remote option
- Requires clearance or citizenship
- "Data Engineer" title without analytics/strategy component
- 10+ years experience required (signals they want a different profile)

## Scoring Guide
- **9-10:** Near-perfect match. Right title, modern stack, strong culture signals. Apply immediately.
- **7-8:** Strong match with minor gaps (e.g., salary not listed, slightly off title). Worth applying.
- **5-6:** Decent match but missing key elements. Flag for manual review.
- **1-4:** Poor fit (wrong level, on-site only, legacy stack, wrong domain requirements). Skip.

## Resume Tier
- **director**: Use for Head/Director/VP roles at smaller companies
- **ic**: Use for Analytics Engineer / Lead / Senior / Staff / Principal roles
